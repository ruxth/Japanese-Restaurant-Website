Sāmon (サーモン)

Delight in the vibrant hues and impeccable freshness of our sashimi-grade salmon. Sourced directly from the icy waters, ensuring an unparalleled sushi experience.

Tai (タイ)

Red Snapper glistening, firm flesh and subtly sweet flavor reflect its recent catch, ensuring each bite is a testament to its unparalleled freshness.

Tako (タコ)

Savor the delicate tenderness of our octopus, expertly crafted to perfection. Its subtly sweet taste showcase the freshness of the sea, offering a delightful contrast to our sushi selection.

Ōtoro (おおとろ)

Indulge in the luxuriousness of our toro (Fatty Tuna), prized for its marbled, velvety texture. I ts richness and tenderness will melt in your mouth.

Maguro (マグロ)

Caught from the deep seas, our tuna is renowned for its delicate flavor. Every bite will deliver a sushi experience that's both fresh and unforgettable.

Sāmon no onaka (サーモンのお腹)

Salmon belly boasts a velvety texture and a rich, reamy flavor profile. Its exquisite marbling and gentle sweetness are a harmonious contrast to the delicate balance of fat and flesh.
